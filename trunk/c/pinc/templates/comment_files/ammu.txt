<p>Regarding receipts:</p>

<p>The receipts are simple to do.  Put one blank line between each receipt,
don't bother to indent (the blank line is sufficient) and mark each donation
amount with a ... before it.</p>

<p>Example:</p>

<pre>RECEIPTS FOR MAY, 1889.

MAINE, $352.06.

Acton. Cong. Ch. and Soc. ...$3.50

Albany. Anna K. Cummings,
  <i>for Mountain Work</i> ...2.00

Bangor. First Cong. Ch. and Soc. ...38.00

Bath. Winter St. Ch. ...140.30

Bucksport. Y.P.S.C.E., by Charlotte
  S. Barnard, <i>for Pleasant Hill, Tenn.</i> ...20.00

Castine. Prof. Fred. W. Foster ...1.44</pre>
