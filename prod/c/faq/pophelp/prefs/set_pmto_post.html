Automatically send your projects to the post-processing pool

You may choose either to have your projects placed automatically in the PP Projects Available pool, or have them assigned directly to you.

This option is read upon the transition of a project from the rounds to PP Available. Changing the option will not affect projects already in PP. Also, this option does not affect projects where a PPer has been assigned on the project page (including if the assigned PPer is yourself).

  • No means your projects will be assigned to you automatically.
  • Yes means your projects will be placed in the PP Projects Available pool.
