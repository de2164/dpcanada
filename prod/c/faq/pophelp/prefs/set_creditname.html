Credit Name
If you want to get credit for one or more of the activities you can be recognized for when a project is posted, you may also be interested in specifying how you would like to be credited.

Real Name will credit your real name (e.g. John Doe).

Username will credit your username (e.g. jdoe).

Other: gives you the opportunity to enter a string on your own. See the PG FAQ for more information.
