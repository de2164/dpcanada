Credits Wanted
When a project gets posted, several people beside the proofreading team can be credited. This means that a line such as "Produced by John Doe, Jane Doe and the Online Distributed Proofreading Team" is included with the final ebook.

You can specify whether or not you would like to be credited for your work in this manner.

CP means "Content Providing" (Deprecated), where we used to record who scanned a book.

IP means "Image Preparing", such as either scanning a book yourself, or harvesting images from an online archive.

TP means "Text Preparing", generating text files from image files, such as through the OCR Pool.

PM means "Project Managing", which means you're sheparding the book through the site (only visible to project managers).

PP means "Post-Processing", which consists of assembling the pages into the final ebook and making e.g. formatting consistent throughout the ebook.
